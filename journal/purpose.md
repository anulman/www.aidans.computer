This purpose statement should be reviewed briefly every day.

The purpose statement will change over time. These reviews are a chance to check in.

When changing the purpose statement, please review and update the following content:

#### What value do I create for others?
I make it easy for cooks of all skill levels to prepare meals of their choosing with confidence

#### I think this is meaningful because:
- It is in every human's best interests to be able to prepare food of their choosing;
- Food & nutrition education is being cut from school programs, and parents appreciate structures and support with teaching life skills; and
- Personal, commercial, and charity / non-profit kitchens all stand to benefit from coordinating cooks of varying experience.

#### Does that value provide societal benefits beyond sales and profits?
Heck yes. We will eat better today, and stand a better chance at surviving zombie apocalypses and extreme weather events tomorrow.
